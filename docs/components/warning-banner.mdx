The warning banner appears in `initialization`, `opening` sensor modes and when temperature, pH or ORP have a measure_mode `maintenance`

- Maintenance warns that data may be outdated.
- Initialization/Opening warns that data may be incomplete.
- The rest of the card is still shown, but it is visually grayed out.
